# The 8 AI Resume Failure Modes (poster)

Printable single-page reference. Companion to [chatgpt-resume-before-after-examples](https://github.com/Sahme115/chatgpt-resume-before-after-examples).

---

| # | Failure mode | Mini example | Fix |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Symmetric rhythm | Every bullet "Led the X of Y" | Vary lead verbs and lengths |
| 2 | Buzzword substitution | leveraged → harnessed | Delete the verb; lead with artifact |
| 3 | Invented metric | "Increased efficiency by 47%" | Anchor to dashboard or delete |
| 4 | Scope inflation | "Led migration" when one of 5 ICs | Use Contributed to / Co-led |
| 5 | Universal summary | "Passionate professional in dynamic environments" | Name role, years, stack, scope |
| 6 | Phantom artifact | "Built v2 platform" (never existed) | Cross-check vs ground truth |
| 7 | Em-dash overload | Bullet — outcome — collaborator | ≤1 per page |
| 8 | Skills as adjectives | "Proficient in cutting-edge tools" | List exact tool names |

---

## Six-second recruiter scan path

1. Most recent title + company
2. Top bullet of most recent role
3. Previous title + company
4. First 1–2 words of every bullet on page 1

Optimize those four; everything else is downstream.

---

## Quick self-check (30s)

- Zero Tier-1 buzzwords on page 1
- No two bullets start with same word
- At least one bullet under 14 words
- Every bullet has one recruiter-askable noun
- No percentage without a source
